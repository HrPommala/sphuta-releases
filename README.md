# Sphuta TMS OSS

**Sphuta TMS OSS** is the open-source edition of the **SPHUTA Timesheet Management System**.

It is designed to support structured time tracking, approvals, operational visibility, reporting, reminders, and export workflows for teams, managers, HR, finance, and business leadership.

## What Sphuta TMS OSS provides

Sphuta TMS OSS helps organizations manage:

- timesheet entry and submission
- approval workflows
- employee and manager visibility
- staffing and assignment oversight
- billing and finance-oriented exports
- operational and executive reporting
- reminders and governance controls

## Core workflow

The standard timesheet lifecycle is:

**Draft → Ready to Submit → Submitted → Under Approval → Approved → Resubmission Required → Rejected → Locked → Archived**

## Main user roles

Sphuta TMS OSS is organized around role-based access for:

- Admin
- HR
- Manager
- Employee
- Finance / Accounts
- Executive / Business Owner

Each role sees its own dashboard, menus, reports, and actions.
