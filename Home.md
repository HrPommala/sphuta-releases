## Product overview

Sphuta TMS OSS supports time tracking and related operational workflows across multiple business roles.

The platform is centered on:
- controlled timesheet submission
- structured approvals
- visibility for managers and leadership
- operational and financial reporting
- configurable reminders and system settings
- role-based access and navigation

## Core status flow

The standard status progression is:

**Draft → Ready to Submit → Submitted → Under Approval → Approved → Resubmission Required → Rejected → Locked → Archived**

## Role model

The platform is organized around the following responsibilities:

- **Admin** — governance, setup, and oversight
- **HR** — people, onboarding, and assignment management
- **Manager** — approvals, team operations, utilization, and reminders
- **Employee** — time entry, submission, corrections, and personal tracking
- **Finance / Accounts** — billing visibility, receivables, payables, exports, and profitability views
- **Executive / Business Owner** — portfolio, financial, and operational oversight
